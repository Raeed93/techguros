Big Data Analyist

Reference: A1122
Position Descriptions:
you will be expected to gather, clean and process big data and find ways to make them into actionable business strategies.
Responsibilities:
  • To gather and accumulate data from disparate sources, clean it, organize it, process it.
  • To identify new sources of data and develop methods to improve data mining, analysis, and reporting.
  • To write SQL queries to extract data from the data warehouse.
  • To present the findings in reports to help the management team in the decision-making process.
Salary:
the entry level salary would be between 80 to 100,000 a year. it could change exponentially with experience and upskilling
